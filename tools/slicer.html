Each cut splits only the region you click in, and stops at that region's edges + earlier cuts (intersections).

· H / V split → click inside a region.
· Angle split → drag across a region (start→end sets the angle).
· Right-click a cut line to remove it (merges back).
· Undo = remove last cut. Reading order = top→bottom, left→right.
· Angled panels export with transparent corners (webp alpha).
After export: unzip into assets/<book-id>/, paste the books[] entry into books.json, set cover. Bump ASSET_V in engine.js if you reuse filenames.
